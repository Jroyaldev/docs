## Create your workspace

When you first sign in to Shepherdly, you'll go through a quick setup process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your church name">
    This appears throughout the app and in team invitations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your timezone">
    Important for event scheduling and sermon delivery dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your accent color">
    Pick a color that matches your church brand. You can change this later in settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After setup, you'll land on your Dashboard with a welcome banner showing quick actions to get started.

## Invite your team

Go to **Team** in the sidebar to invite members.

1. Click **Invite member**
2. Choose their role:
   - **Pastor** — Full access to all workspaces
   - **Staff** — Access to operations and people workspaces
   - **Volunteer** — Limited access, can create tasks and prayers
3. Copy the invite link or send via email

Team members use the link to join your workspace. Their role determines what they can access.

## Create your first sermon

Head to **Sermons** and click **New sermon**.

1. Enter a title
2. Optionally add scripture references and a scheduled delivery date
3. Click **Create**

Your sermon starts in the "Idea" status. Open it in the Studio editor to start writing.

<Tip>
The sermon workflow moves through: Idea → Outlining → Writing → Polishing → Ready → Delivered
</Tip>

## Add upcoming events

Go to **Calendar** and click on a date or use **New event**.

- Set the title, date, and time
- Choose visibility: church-wide, team-only, or personal
- Add a location if applicable
- Toggle "All-day" for events without specific times

Events appear on your Dashboard and in the calendar view.

## Set up your people directory

The **People** workspace is where you track your congregation.

1. Go to **People > Directory**
2. Click **Add person**
3. Enter their name, contact info, and any relevant notes
4. Assign them to a household if applicable

You can also organize people into **Groups** (small groups, ministries) and **Classes** (educational programs).

<Note>
The People workspace is restricted to leadership roles (lead pastor, pastor, staff) to protect sensitive pastoral information.
</Note>
